About me

I am a certified teacher from Ontario, Canada. I am incredibly passionate about making education accessible, engaging and fun for all students, regardless of their abilities, background, or interests!

I have a Bachelors Degree (honours specialization) in Psychology from Nipissing University, a Bachelors Degree in Education from Queen's University, and a Teaching English as a Foreign Language Certificate from the University of Toronto.



Learning Should be Fun!


Education can be enjoyable for every single student, provided that they have a teacher who makes a dedicated effort to tailor their education to each student's personal interests and learning preferences.
